Lead Inspector's call to the ITE partnership

Why do I need to call the ITE partnership?

After the ISA notifies the partnership about the upcoming inspection, it is the Lead Inspector's duty to hold a planning call with them. There are two areas you need to discuss with the partnership:

  • education focused conversation
  • inspection planning conversation

1. The inspection process

During the phone call, you will review:

  • details of trainees’ placements or place of work, including addresses and unique reference numbers (URNs) for schools, colleges or other settings
  • discuss meeting with trainees currently on the training course.

2. The education environment

As Lead Inspector, you need to understand:

  • The partnership’s context
  • the progress it has made since the previous inspection
  • assessment of its current strengths and weaknesses
  • any specific progress made on areas for improvement identified at previous inspections that remain relevant under the current inspection framework Dummy link

You will also need to understand:

  • how trainees are taught to encourage positive behaviour and attitudes in pupils
  • how those with special educational needs and disabilities (SEND) are supported throughout the ITE curriculums offered.
