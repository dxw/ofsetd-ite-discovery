Prepare the inspection plan

The ITE partnerships work with different education settings where trainees practice their teaching. As part of the ITE inspections, inspectors observe trainees in these settings.

As there might be various locations for the inspection team to visit, the Lead Inspector is responsible for putting together a plan for the team to follow throughout the inspection.

When creating this plan, the Lead Inspector has to bear in mind the following conditions:

  • Are there limitations that might prevent any inspectors from going into any of the education settings this ITE partnership works with?
  • How can I select a sample of trainees to observe that is representative of their traineeship routes and their specialist phases?
  • What's the best route for the inspection team to follow to make those visits?

You can also use this guidance when you select your sample trainees.
